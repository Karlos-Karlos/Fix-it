How the AI Works

We use MediaPipe Pose — a free, browser-based AI — to detect your body landmarks and estimate composition. For best results:

  • ✓ Full body visible head to toe
  • ✓ Face the camera directly
  • ✓ Good lighting, plain background
  • ✓ Enter your height & weight for accuracy

All analysis runs locally in your browser. Your photo is never uploaded.
